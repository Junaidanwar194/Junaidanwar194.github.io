About Me

Currently, I am a Ph.D candidate at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Tennessee Technological University, USA . I received my B.Sc. degree from the University of Engineering and Technology (UET) Taxila, Pakistan in 2012 and, my M.S. degree in Electrical Engineering from the National University of Science and Technology, Islamabad, Pakistan in 2017. I'm a recipient of the 2023 Ph.D. Scholarship Award at Tennessee Tech University. I have also presented my poster at the American Control Conference (ACC) held in San Diego, USA, 2023. My current research interests are reinforcement learning, autonomous vehicles, building HVAC control, and energy system control.
